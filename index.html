about
Hi, I'm Long — founder of Do Tattoo and Ngoa Studio, where stories are not just told, but quietly worn.
My approach is unique — every tattoo is not just ink on skin, but a living, evolving piece of art that reflects his client's individuality.
Dedicated to perfection in every detail, I view each tattoo as a personal journey. My commitment to excellence goes beyond technique, focusing on creating meaningful pieces that are as enduring as they are visually striking.
From Do to Ngoa
DoTattoo Ngoa
Ngoa was born not as a separate path, but as an evolution — a quieter chapter, rooted in the same hands that shaped Đô Tattoo Studio. Created by Long, founder of both spaces, Ngoa carries the same spirit of precision and presence, but moves deeper into silence, stillness, and soul.
Where Đô marked stories on skin, Ngoa listens for the space between words — continuing the same devotion, in a softer voice.
